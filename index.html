Our Story

Rooted in Culture.
Growing Together.

GRACE (Greater Roslyn Association for Chinese Enrichment) was built on a simple but powerful idea: when families feel connected, supported, and heard, an entire community becomes stronger. As a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization, we are proud to serve Chinese American families in Roslyn by creating a space where culture is celebrated, voices are valued, and relationships can flourish.

Our mission is not only to support families, but to help them feel a true sense of belonging. Through education, community service, and civic engagement, GRACE brings people together, encourages participation, and helps families become more connected to the life of our town. We serve as a bridge between Chinese families, local government, the school district, and the broader Roslyn community, building understanding and creating opportunities for everyone to grow together.

We believe that by sharing information, creating meaningful programs, and bringing families together, we can enrich lives in lasting ways. GRACE is more than an organization — it is a community built on care, connection, and hope for the future. Together, we can create a Roslyn where every family feels welcomed, empowered, and at home.

Who We Are

Founded in 2019,
Serving Roslyn.

GRACE — Greater Roslyn Association for Chinese Enrichment — is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to serving Chinese American families in Roslyn, New York. We were founded in 2019 on the belief that culture, community, and connection are essential to a thriving life.

Our mission is not only to support families, but to help them feel a true sense of belonging. Through education, community service, and civic engagement, GRACE brings people together, encourages participation, and helps families become more connected to the life of our town.

We serve as a bridge between Chinese families, local government, the school district, and the broader Roslyn community — building understanding and creating opportunities for everyone to grow together.

Mission

Enriching Lives Through Culture & Connection

To create a welcoming, inclusive community where Chinese American families in Roslyn can celebrate their heritage, strengthen their roots, and build meaningful connections with neighbors of all backgrounds.

Vision

A Roslyn Where Every Family Belongs

We envision a Roslyn where every family — regardless of background — feels welcomed, empowered, and at home. A community where cultural diversity is celebrated as a source of collective strength and pride.

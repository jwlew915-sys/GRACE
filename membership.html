Why Join

Member Benefits

🎉
Event Access

Priority access and early invitations to all GRACE events, including the annual Lunar New Year Gala and Mid-Autumn Festival.

🤝
Community Network

Connect with hundreds of Chinese American families across Roslyn — neighbors, parents, professionals, and community leaders.

📚
Program Participation

Enroll your family in heritage language classes, cultural workshops, and youth programs offered by GRACE throughout the year.

📣
Civic Voice

Add your voice to GRACE's advocacy efforts — ensuring Chinese American perspectives are heard in Roslyn schools and government.

📰
News & Resources

Receive our newsletter with community news, school district updates, cultural resources, and upcoming event announcements.

🌟
Support Our Mission

Your membership directly funds the programs and events that serve our community. Be part of something meaningful and lasting.

Membership

Ready to Join?

GRACE membership is open to all families and individuals who share our commitment to community, culture, and connection. Whether you're Chinese American or a friend of the community, you are welcome here.

Fill out the form and a GRACE team member will be in touch to complete your membership and welcome you to the family.

Our Promise to You

GRACE is a safe, inclusive, and welcoming community. We will never share your information with third parties, and membership is always free to join.
